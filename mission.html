Our Mission

Democratizing underwater exploration through affordable, accessible mapping technology

Why We Exist

Over 80% of the ocean remains unmapped and unexplored. Lakes, rivers, and coastal regions hold countless mysteries, yet traditional underwater mapping requires expensive ROVs, specialized vessels, and expert operators‚Äîputting this technology out of reach for most researchers, conservationists, and explorers.

MapTheSea was founded on a simple belief: underwater exploration should be accessible to everyone. We're building autonomous underwater vehicles that use commodity sensors, 3D-printed components, and open-source software to map underwater environments at a fraction of traditional costs.

Our mission extends beyond technology. We're creating a platform that enables scientists to study marine ecosystems, helps search-and-rescue teams locate missing persons, allows infrastructure inspectors to assess underwater structures, and empowers curious explorers to discover what lies beneath the surface.
