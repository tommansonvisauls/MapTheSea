Our Mission

Creating the world's first affordable underwater mapping platform

The ocean floor, lake beds, and coastal waters remain largely unmapped. Traditional underwater mapping requires expensive equipment and specialized vessels, making comprehensive mapping impossible for most applications.

MapTheSea is developing autonomous underwater drones that use simple sensors, cameras, and GPS buoys to create detailed maps at a fraction of traditional costs. Our early-stage prototypes demonstrate the viability of democratizing underwater exploration.

We're in active R&D, testing propulsion systems and sensor integration to build scalable, modular underwater mapping vehicles that anyone can deploy.

How It Works

Simple technology. Powerful results.

1

Underwater Drone

Autonomous vehicle navigates beneath the surface using onboard propulsion and stabilization systems.

2

GPS Buoy

Surface buoy maintains GPS connection, providing precise location data for underwater positioning.

3

Sensor Array

Camera, depth sensor, and temperature probe capture environmental data at regular intervals.

4

Map Generation

Data is processed and stitched into comprehensive underwater maps using photogrammetry algorithms.
